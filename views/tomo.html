Tomography Installation

Computed tomography systems that provide fast and detailed cross -sectional imaging installation Services.

Planning and Preparation

Evaluation of the room where the tomography system will be installed, radiation security Making the necessary arrangements for taking precautions and placement of the device.

Correct planning allows the installation to progress efficiently and safely.

Device assembly and integration

Installation of the main units of the tomography device, high voltage and data cables The integration of connections and imaging systems are critical steps.

Our experienced technical team performs these operations with precision.

Test, Adjustment and User Training

System performance tests, image quality settings and radiation after installation Dosimetry checks are performed. Finally, comprehensive education for user staff given.

Our aim is to provide a smooth start and long -term reliable use.
